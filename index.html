The simplest watchface ever made on your hand


Get Watchface
on Github

Simple watchface that displays a text instead of time. You may customize it to have your own text, or even motivational small phrases. However, if you want to see the time, just shake your hand.
Tweet

You can customize the text displayed on your Pebble, change the display between 12h and 24h time format and also change the timeout value for time.

Here are some advantages:
  • Enjoy yourself using a customized message
  • You can see the time by shaking yor wrist
  • The watchface can save your battery
